INFORMATION OF THE USER

Once the car desired and the days for the renting are selected, the user must validate the renting by giving the following mandatory fields:
- Name and Surname (this field must not be blank nor empty)
- Telephone Number (this field must not be blank nor empty and the number of digits must be exactly 9)
- Date of the Renting (the month, day and year when the rent starts must be introduced)
Once all this fields had been completed and validated, the user must press the button "confirm" to confirm the rental. If the user is not sure about the renting, he can go back at any moment ("before confirming the rent") by pressing the "previous" button, which will lead him to the window were the information of the car is shown.
